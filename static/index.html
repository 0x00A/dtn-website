Details

What to Expect

Expect subversive and unconventional ideas that will challenge your thinking. Ideas focused on implementations. A small event designed to promote constructive discussions between everyone involved. Expect a group of people who share an interest in securing the freedom of information & how we build systems around it.

What's Included

This is a not-for-profit event. All sponsorship pays for the conference itself. Aside from discussions and talks, attendees can expect delicious food and coffee. All talks and interviews will be recorded and published online after the event.
